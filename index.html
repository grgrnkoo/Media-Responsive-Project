Vietnam Cycling Club

We unite all cyclists around Viet Nam

Started as a group of university friends, cycling after classes, we grew into largest community in great country of Viet Nam!

Now VCC counts more than thousand cyclists, meeting for a ride each week, using our platform. Also we provide rental services in three cities. This cities are tourists' beloved destinations: Ho Chi Minh, Ha Noi and Da Nang.

Tourists love to take a single ride through the city or countryside. Some of them join our weekly rides and get unique experience.

Viet Nam is known for it's beautiful nature an all-year comfortable weather. So bicycles are favourite transport for vietnamese guys, giving way only to a motorbikes.

We all know, that bicycle has no competitors, when it comes to healthy lifestyle
